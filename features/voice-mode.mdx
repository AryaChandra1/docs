Emergent has always been built on a simple belief - the best interface is the one that **gets out of your way**.

We started by removing the need to write code.\
Now, we’re removing the need to even type.

Introducing **Voice Mode** - the most natural way to build apps with Emergent.\
Speak your ideas out loud, and Emergent will turn your words into production-ready applications in real time.

---

<Info>
  Voice Mode lets you **talk to Emergent** as naturally as you’d explain your idea to a teammate.\
  You describe it - Emergent builds it.
</Info>

---

## What Is Voice Mode?

Voice Mode is the **next step in natural development** - enabling you to:

- **Describe your app in plain English**
- **Iterate hands-free**, anywhere
- **Build faster** while staying in your creative flow

You can brainstorm ideas while walking your dog, tweak your UI over coffee, or launch an entire MVP without touching your keyboard.

It’s fast. It’s intuitive.\
It feels like **talking your idea into existence**.

<Tip>
  Voice Mode is available on both **Web and Mobile** - so you can build anytime inspiration strikes.
</Tip>

---

## How to Use Voice Mode

1. **Open Emergent** (on web or mobile)
2. Tap the **microphone icon** in the bottom toolbar
3. **Start speaking** - describe what you want to build
4. Watch as Emergent **translates your words into live code and UI**

<Check>
  You can switch seamlessly between **voice and typing** at any time - Emergent keeps up with your flow.
</Check>
